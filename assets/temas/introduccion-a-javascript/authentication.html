  • StackOverflow: How to Test Facebook Connect Locally

    • Facebook → Settings → Basic:

      write “localhost” in the “App Domains” field then click on “+Add Platform” choose “Web Site”.

      After that, in the “Site Url” field write your localhost url (e.g.: https://localhost:3000/login/facebook/return). This will allow you to test your facebook plugins locally.
